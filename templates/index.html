About This Platform

Welcome to the Caregiver Platform - a comprehensive solution for connecting caregivers with families in need of care services.

Features:
  • User Management: Register and manage users, caregivers, and family members
  • Job Postings: Members can post job advertisements for caregiver positions
  • Applications: Caregivers can apply for jobs that match their skills
  • Appointments: Schedule and manage caregiving appointments
  • Search & Filter: Find caregivers by type, city, and other criteria
